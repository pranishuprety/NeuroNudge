Mindful Nudges. Parental Control. Digital Wellbeing.

NeuroNudge is a Chromium-based extension that helps users stay balanced by reducing distraction, overworking, and digital overload. It adapts to your browsing rhythm, spotting small signs of drift and gently guiding you back before focus fades. For parents, the Parent Mode adds smart guardrails that help children build healthy screen habits, especially on school Chromebooks widely used from middle school through high school. When deeper support is needed, the Amazon Nova ACT assistant provides personalized routines that restore focus and flow.

Why we built NeuroNudge

We built NeuroNudge to help people build a healthier relationship with technology. Screens are part of nearly every moment of modern life, and it is easy to lose focus, overwork, or drift without realizing it. NeuroNudge helps you stay centered by sensing when your attention starts to fade, guiding you back to focus, and reminding you to take mindful breaks before exhaustion sets in. It is designed for anyone who wants to work or study with purpose, and for parents who want to create balance and safety for their kids online.

Parent Mode keeps habits healthy

Set clear guardrails for the sites that drain focus. Parent Mode lives entirely on the device, giving families Chromebook-ready controls without new accounts or dashboards. Keep social tabs closed during class and let curiosity roam again once the school day wraps. Nova routines add creative breaks when kids (or parents) need a reset.

Block the big distractions

Choose sites that stay off-limits while Parent Mode is on. NeuroNudge gently redirects to a calming reminder page if they pop up‚Äîideal for shared family laptops and managed school Chromebooks.

Time-box screen time

Add daily caps or per-session timers for specific sites. When the limit is reached, the browser pauses that destination until it resets, encouraging breaks, stretches, and real-world conversation. Nova can even lead a short reset ritual before letting students hop back in.

Install NeuroNudge today

While we finish Chrome Web Store review, you can sideload the extension in developer mode. Expect the store listing soon!

  1. Download or clone the project from GitHub.
  2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions.
  3. Enable Developer mode in the top right.
  4. Choose Load unpacked and select the downloaded NeuroNudge folder.
  5. Pin the extension and start exploring mindful productivity nudges.
